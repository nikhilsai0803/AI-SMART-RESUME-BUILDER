About ResumeLens AI

An AI-powered resume platform built to level the playing field for every job seeker.

The Vision

Most job seekers never know why their resume got filtered out. ATS systems silently reject qualified candidates based on keywords, formatting, and structure — not talent. ResumeLens AI was built to change that: give everyone the same feedback that expensive career coaches charge for, instantly, for free.

ATS Scoring

ML-powered compatibility score using a TF-IDF model trained on real job descriptions. Section-level breakdown for Contact, Skills, Experience, and Education.

Resume Builder

Structured multi-section builder that exports a properly formatted DOCX file — ready to send to recruiters without any extra editing.

AI Feedback

Gemini-powered suggestions on every section of your resume — what's working, what's weak, and specific rewrites to make it stronger.

Skill Gap Analysis

Compare your resume against any target role. See exactly which skills you're missing and get a prioritised list of what to learn next.

Interview Prep

Generate role-specific interview questions from your resume. Practise answers before you walk into the room.

Recruiter Screening

Bulk upload 50+ resumes, define criteria (education, skills, ATS threshold), and get an AI-ranked shortlist with CSV export in seconds.

Privacy by design

Every resume you upload is parsed in memory and deleted immediately after analysis. Nothing is stored on disk, nothing is shared. No tracking, no analytics, no ads.
